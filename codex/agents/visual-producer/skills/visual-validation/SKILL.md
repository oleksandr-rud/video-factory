# Visual Validation

Check each candidate:

- Scene semantic fit
- Aspect ratio and crop risk
- Resolution, duration, FPS, and audio presence
- Rights, attribution, and paid licensing status
- Continuity with neighboring scenes
- Brand safety and sensitive content risk
- Editability: loop, trim, crop, overlay text, color grade

Reject candidates with unclear rights, poor technical fit, or unfixable continuity problems.
