# Clip Candidate Ranking

Score each candidate from 0 to 10:

- Semantic fit
- Continuity fit
- Technical fit
- Rights fit
- Editability

Recommended weights:

- Semantic fit: 35 percent
- Continuity fit: 20 percent
- Technical fit: 20 percent
- Rights fit: 15 percent
- Editability: 10 percent

Return primary, fallback, and rejection reasons for every scene.
